What We Do

The Anoka Baseball Booster Club’s purpose is to advance the standing of the Anoka High School baseball teams within the community by promoting and stimulating fan support and interest in the game of baseball. The Booster Club was established to provide financial support through fundraising efforts which compliments the Anoka High School’s budget commitment to the program. Throughout the season, the booster club raises money to buy equipment, uniforms and pay coach salaries. Our fundraisers include a letter writing campaign in the fall and selling the Castle Card in the spring. With these fundraisers and other donations we hope to raise nearly $40,000 to support the program. The booster club also supports several events during the season including Youth Night (freshmen parents), End-of-Season Team Banquet (sophomore parents) and Senior Night (junior parents).
