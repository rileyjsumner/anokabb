Summer Registration Process

ALL PLAYERS planning to play summer traveling baseball (after the High School season), must register with AR Baseball by April 27. The ONLY exception are players that played on the Legion team in 2015. All others must register via the link below. This registration is for all potential 15AAA, 15AA, VFW, 16AAA and 19U traveling baseball players. Final team rosters will be formed and communicated in mid-May based on registrations. Players invited to the Legion team that have registered with AR baseball, will receive a refund. Click the link below to get started. $225 registration can be paid in one or two payments.
